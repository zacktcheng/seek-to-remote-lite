Seek to Remote Lite is a lightweight web application to explore and recommend remote jobs all over the world.

There're 15+ occupation categories in the drop down menu(with the bars icon). Buttons mentioned below take what's been selected to get the job posts. Click the post to check out the job description, click the hyperlink to open job application page in another browser, or click the star button to save the job post.

Explore Openings(with the binoculars icon):
The application searches 20 newest job posts from Remotive.io then provides them in a list.

I'm Feeling Lucky(with the smiling face icon):
The application searches job posts from Remotive.io in bulk, then it randomly picks 20 job posts among them. If you Like one then save it, chances are that you may not see the same job post again ever.

My Favorites(with the star icon):
The application shows all job posts you've saved. To remove one, just simply click its star button again. Please note that having insufficient favorites can cause the application to recommend 0 job post.

Recommendation(with the light bulb icon):
The application searches job posts from Remotive.io in bulk, then compares the similarity between input job posts and your saved job posts in 'My Favorites' to determine recommended jobs for you.

***IMPORTANT*** : Be sure to logout before closing the tab / browser, otherwise your latest changes may not be saved.
