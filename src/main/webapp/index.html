About:

Seek to Remote Lite, as its name implies, is a lite weight remote job recommendation web application.

There're 15+ categories in the drop down menu(the uppermost left). Buttons mentioned below take what's been selected to get the job posts. Click the post to check out the job description, click the hyperlink to open job application page in another browser, or click the star button to save the job post.

Explore Openings:
By clicking Explore Openings button, the application searches 10 newest job posts from Remotive.io then provide them in a list.

I'm Feeling Lucky:
By clicking I'm Feeling Lucky button, the application searches job posts from Remotive.io in bulk, then it randomly picks 10 job posts among them. If you Like one then save it, chances are that you may not see the same job post again ever.

My Favorites:
By clicking My Favorites button, the application shows all job posts you've saved. To remove one, just simply click its star button again. Please note that having insufficient favorites can cause the application to recommend 0 job post.

Recommendation:
By clicking Recommendation button, the application searches job posts from Remotive.io in bulk, then compares the similarity between input job posts and your saved job posts in 'My Favorites' to determine recommended jobs for you.

***IMPORTANT***: Be sure to logout before closing the tab / browser, otherwise your latest changes may not be saved.
